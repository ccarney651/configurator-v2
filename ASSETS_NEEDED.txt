Free GLB model sources:
  Sketchfab (sketchfab.com)           — large library, filter by license
  Poly Pizza (poly.pizza)             — low-poly, CC0 licensed
  KitBash3D (kitbash3d.com)           — higher quality, paid
  BlenderKit (blenderkit.com)         — Blender add-on, free tier available

Free tileable texture sources:
  Polyhaven (polyhaven.com)           — CC0, excellent quality
  Ambientcg.com                       — CC0, wide variety
  Texturecan.com                      — free with attribution

GLB requirements:
  - Exported at real-world scale (1 unit = 1 metre)
  - Y-axis up
  - Origin at bottom-left-front corner (consistent with existing door/window models)
  - Baked or PBR materials included in the GLB
  - Target polygon count: <10,000 triangles per piece of furniture
  - Test with: https://gltf-viewer.donmccurdy.com/
